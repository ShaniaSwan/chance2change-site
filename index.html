Our Mission

Chance2Change exists to empower people with faith, skills, and opportunity so they can rebuild their lives, support their families, and strengthen their communities. Through trade training, mentoring, real estate redevelopment, and professional services, we create pathways for second chances and long-term change.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a movement where people once overlooked become the builders of stronger futures. We aim to grow into community academies, training spaces, and partnerships that provide housing, hands-on projects, mentorship, and practical skills that restore neighborhoods and lives.
