Our story

From second chances to new beginnings.

Chance2Change was born from a real journey of struggle, faith, resilience, and opportunity. Gregg Swan grew up watching his family struggle to pay rent, but that experience sparked a dream to one day own property and build something bigger.

Through skilled trades training, real estate experience, property rehabilitation, and years of hands-on work, that dream became a mission: teach others the same practical skills that can change lives.
